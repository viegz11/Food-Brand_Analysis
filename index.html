Objectives

Key goals of the customer sentiment analysis project

  • Integrate structured and unstructured data from multiple sources
  • Clean and standardize feedback data for consistency
  • Perform sentiment analysis to categorize feedback as Positive, Neutral, or Negative
  • Analyze product performance across key attributes
  • Segment customers into actionable personas based on behavior
  • Provide strategic recommendations for product improvement and marketing

Data Sources

Customer feedback collected from structured surveys and unstructured social media reviews

Structured Data (Survey)

Collected through customer surveys capturing:

  • Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Location)
  • Product details (Brand, Food Item, Food Type)
  • Ratings for Taste, Price, Packaging, Food Quality, and Overall Feedback
  • Purchase behavior and recommendation intent

Unstructured Data (Social Media)

Customer reviews collected from Facebook(Apify Scraper), including:

  • Customer ID
  • Product Name
  • Free-text customer feedback

Data Cleaning & Standardization

Converting raw and noisy data into analysis-ready datasets

  • Standardized column names and formats across all survey and review files
  • Removed duplicate records and invalid entries to ensure data accuracy
  • Handled missing and null values using logical data rules
  • Converted all text fields to lowercase for consistency
  • Cleaned special characters, emojis, and extra spaces from text feedback
  • Unified Customer ID and Product ID for seamless dataset integration

Sentiment Analysis

NLP-based sentiment extraction from customer feedback text

Customer feedback text from surveys and social media was analyzed using NLP techniques. TextBlob polarity scores were calculated for each feedback entry and mapped to sentiment labels.

  • Sentiment Score: Numerical polarity value ranging from -1 (Negative) to +1 (Positive)
  • Sentiment Label: Classified as Positive, Neutral, or Negative using polarity thresholds
  • Emotion Category: Derived from sentiment patterns such as satisfaction, complaint, or neutral feedback
  • Text-based sentiment was combined with rating-based sentiment for final analysis

Positive

Positive feedback indicating customer satisfaction and delight

Examples: "Amazing burger!", "Best coffee ever"

Neutral

Neutral feedback with no strong positive or negative sentiment

Examples: "Food was okay", "Standard service"

Negative

Negative feedback requiring immediate attention and action

Examples: "Cold pizza", "Rude service"

Sentiment Insights

Business insights derived from sentiment analysis dashboards

  • Overall Sentiment Health: Analysis of 161K+ feedback records shows a strong positive sentiment dominance, with an average sentiment score of 77.07%.
  • Positive Sentiment: Reflects customer satisfaction driven mainly by taste, food quality, and brand experience.
  • Neutral Sentiment: Represents customers whose expectations are met but not exceeded — a key opportunity segment for conversion.
  • Negative Sentiment: Highlights concerns around pricing, packaging quality, service delays, and unmet expectations.
  • Trend & Regional Analysis: Sentiment trends over time and city-wise comparison reveal location-specific strengths and improvement areas.
  • Business Impact: Enables proactive quality control, targeted regional strategies, and early detection of customer dissatisfaction.

Product Insights

Analyzing product performance across key attributes

This dashboard evaluates product performance by combining customer ratings, sentiment scores, and feedback volume to identify both high-performing and underperforming products.

Key Product Attributes Analyzed

Taste & Flavor – strongest driver of positive sentiment
Price Value – key contributor to negative sentiment when misaligned
Food Quality – directly linked to high ratings
Packaging – major improvement opportunity
Preparation Time – impacts repeat purchase behavior

Analysis Features

Compare products using average sentiment score and ratings
Identify high-volume products with declining sentiment (risk zones)
Analyze product strength index (overall score = 0.68)
Support data-driven decisions for pricing, packaging, and quality improvements

Customer Segmentation Insights

Grouping customers into actionable personas based on behavior

This dashboard segments customers using sentiment, ratings, purchasing behavior, and engagement levels to support personalized marketing and retention strategies.

Positive Users

  • Consistently positive sentiment and ratings
  • Represent the largest customer segment
  • Strong candidates for referrals and advocacy
Action: Referral programs, testimonials

Critics

  • High negative sentiment but valuable feedback
  • Identify quality, pricing, and service gaps
Action: Issue resolution, quality improvement loops

Silent Users

  • Regular buyers with minimal feedback
  • Major conversion opportunity segment
Action: Feedback nudges, in-app surveys

Loyal Fans

  • High sentiment but low engagement frequency
  • Only ~10.86% of total customers
Action: Loyalty programs, exclusive offers
